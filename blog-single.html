VETRA Launches New Road Safety Campaign

August 9, 2025 | By VETRA News Desk

In a bid to improve road safety and reduce the number of traffic accidents in the city, the VETRA (Kano Road Traffic Agency) has launched a comprehensive road safety awareness campaign. The program targets drivers, pedestrians, and commercial transport operators with the goal of promoting responsible road usage.

This campaign will involve a series of workshops, distribution of educational materials, and regular social media updates to engage the public. The initiative is part of VETRA's long-term strategy to create safer streets and ensure the smooth flow of traffic.

"Our mission is to save lives by promoting a culture of safety and respect on the roads." — VETRA Executive Director

The campaign will also include collaboration with schools, local communities, and commercial drivers’ unions to ensure widespread awareness. VETRA has urged all road users to participate and adopt safer driving habits.
